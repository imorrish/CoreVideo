Standalone app for online conversation production

Produce polished live conversations, no OBS required.

CoreVideo Pro is a standalone Windows app that turns a Zoom call into a multi-scene, multi-camera production — with participant management, streaming and recording outputs, and an AI auto-director that keeps the show moving, so your team can focus on the conversation instead of the controls.

See what it does Compare with CoreVideo (OBS Plugin)
Multi-Scene ProductionIntro, interview, speaker-plus-slides, panel, and closing scene templates with Cut/Fade/Slide transitions and Take.
Participant ManagementLive Zoom roster with Host, Presenter, Panelist, and Guest roles, manual scene-slot assignment, and per-participant audio and video controls.
Streaming & RecordingProgram and ISO recording, 1080p/4K output profiles, and multi-destination RTMP/NDI/SRT streaming with preflight checks.
AI Auto-DirectMagic Scene and Set & Forget automatically recommend and take scene layouts from live Zoom activity, so a show can run itself.

How it fits with CoreVideo

CoreVideo Pro is a separate, standalone product from the CoreVideo OBS plugin. Both connect to Zoom for raw participant media, but they're built for different workflows:

ProductForm factorBest for
CoreVideo (OBS Plugin)Plugin inside OBS StudioOperators who already run shows in OBS and want Zoom participants as native sources, ISO recording, and an Active Speaker Director.
CoreVideo Pro (Standalone App)Standalone Windows appProducers who want a dedicated, ready-to-go console for online conversations — scenes, participants, outputs, and AI auto-direct in one app, without configuring OBS.
